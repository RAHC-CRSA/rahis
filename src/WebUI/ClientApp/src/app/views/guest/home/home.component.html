RAHIS
Regional Animal Health Information System

Regional Animal Health Information System (RAHIS) is a web based, remotely accessible tool for surveillance, investigation and information exchange on regional Transboundary Animal Diseases (TADs) and zoonoses.

View report Learn more

RAHIS is an initiative of the Regional Animal Health Center (RAHC), an intergovernmental agency assigned the mandate of providing an essential framework to monitor, aggregate, evaluate and analyse data pertaining to emerging diseases affecting animals in West Africa.
